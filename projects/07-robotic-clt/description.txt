ONGOING RESEARCH INTO AUTOMATION OF ROBOTIC ASSEMBLY OF STRUCTURALLY OPTIMIZED CROSS-LAMINATED TIMBER PANELS USING TIMBER OFFCUTS. SIMULATED STRUCTURAL LOADS GENERATE INDIVIDUAL PANEL VOXEL FIELDS FROM VERTICAL, LATERAL, AND TORSIONAL LOADS. A ROBOT-MOUNTED SCHMALZ VACUUM AREA GRIPPER LIFTS, POSITIONS, AND SETS THE PLANKS IN PLACE.
